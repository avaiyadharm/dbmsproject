Our Story

Maximum rent-a-car was founded in 2010 with a single vehicle and big dreams. Today, we are a leading regional rent-a-car provider with over 1000 vehicles in our fleet.

Our vision was clear - to redefine the car rental experience through transparency, premium service, and luxury vehicles.

We strive daily to realize that vision, and your trust and satisfaction are our greatest reward.
