Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to your questions.

What are the branch operating hours?

Our branches are open Monday to Friday from 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM, and Saturdays from 9:00 AM to 3:00 PM. Branches are closed on Sundays and public holidays, but you can contact us by phone or via the website.

Can I pick up the vehicle outside of working hours?

Yes, we can arrange after-hours vehicle pickup with prior notice and for an additional fee of 25 EUR. Please contact us at least 24 hours in advance so we can organize the pickup.

How can I change or cancel a reservation?

Reservation changes and cancellations are possible via email or phone. To cancel without a fee, you must notify us at least 24 hours before vehicle pickup. For cancellations within less than 24 hours, a fee equal to 50% of the total rental price will be charged.

When will I receive a reply to my inquiry?

We strive to respond to all inquiries within 24 hours. Responses may be slightly slower during weekends and holidays. For urgent inquiries, please contact us by phone.
