Rental Terms

Get informed about the rules and conditions for renting a vehicle with Maximum rent-a-car.

Key Terms at a Glance

The most important information in one place.

Minimum Rental

The minimum rental duration is 24 hours. Short-term rentals are available for a special fee.

Age Limits

Rental permitted for persons aged 21 and older with a minimum of 2 years of driving experience.

Deposit

The deposit ranges from 500 to 1500 EUR depending on the vehicle category. It is fully refunded after the vehicle return.

Insurance

All vehicles have basic comprehensive insurance with deductible (excess). Full insurance is available for an additional fee.

Fuel

The vehicle is rented with a full tank and must be returned with a full tank.

Return Time

Delay in returning the vehicle after the agreed time will be charged as an additional rental day.

Detailed Rental Conditions

Everything you need to know before booking a vehicle.

1. General Conditions

All users of Maximum rent-a-car services are obliged to adhere to these rental conditions. The rental agreement enters into force upon signature by the contracting parties and is valid for the specified period of vehicle use.

Rentals can be made online via our website or directly at our branches. A down payment of 30% of the total rental amount is required to confirm the reservation.

2. Renter's Obligations

The renter is obliged to:

  • Use the vehicle in accordance with its purpose and technical capabilities
  • Comply with legal provisions on traffic safety
  • Not transfer the vehicle to third parties without the written permission of Maximum rent-a-car
  • Immediately report any damage to the vehicle or traffic accident
  • Return the vehicle within the agreed period and in the condition in which it was received

3. Prices and Payment

Rental prices include:

  • Unlimited mileage
  • Basic vehicle insurance
  • 24/7 roadside assistance
  • VAT according to applicable rates

Additional fees may apply for:

  • Additional drivers
  • Crossing state borders (upon request)
  • Child seats
  • GPS navigation

4. Insurance and Liability

All vehicles are insured according to legal provisions. The renter bears financial responsibility for all damage to the vehicle and third parties resulting from:

  • Using the vehicle under the influence of alcohol or narcotics
  • Unauthorized driving
  • Using the vehicle outside the agreed conditions
  • Non-compliance with traffic regulations

5. Reservation Cancellation

Reservations can be canceled free of charge no later than 24 hours before the planned vehicle pickup. For cancellations within less than 24 hours, a fee equal to 50% of the total rental price will be charged.

Maximum rent-a-car reserves the right to cancel the reservation in case of force majeure or technical problems with the vehicle, with a full refund of the paid amount.

Frequently Asked Questions

Answers to the most common questions related to vehicle rental.

What is the deposit for vehicle rental?

The deposit depends on the vehicle category and ranges from 500 to 1500 EUR. The exact deposit amount will be shown to you during the reservation process. The deposit is fully refunded after the vehicle is inspected and determined to have no damage.

What is included in the rental price?

The basic rental price includes unlimited mileage, basic vehicle insurance (comprehensive with deductible), 24/7 roadside assistance, and VAT. Additional services such as an extra driver, GPS navigation, or child seats are charged separately.

Can I cross the border with the rented vehicle?

Yes, international rental is possible with prior notice and an additional fee for crossing the border. A list of permitted countries is available at our branches. An additional agreement must be signed before crossing the border.

What if a traffic accident occurs?

In the event of a traffic accident, contact us immediately at the emergency number listed in the rental agreement. Before leaving the scene of the accident, be sure to collect information about other participants and request a police report. Do not repair anything on the vehicle without our authorization.

Can I extend the vehicle rental?

Yes, you can extend the rental by contacting us at least 24 hours before the expiry of the current rental agreement. Extension is possible with our consent and depends on vehicle availability. An additional fee will be charged for extending the rental beyond the initially agreed period.

Still have questions?

Our team is always available to provide you with further clarification and assistance.
